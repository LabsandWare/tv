Business Planning, Budgeting, Forecasting and Project Feasibility Studies

Proper feasibility studies and analysis of any proposed project before commitment to invest and undertake is the key to success and to ensuring that any business undertaking with a large or potentially high risk initiative delivers both the business objectives and required business benefits.
Our team of experts will ensure that your projects are fully evaluated and subjected to thorough analysis to determine their viability and practicability and the operational and technical feasibility of available solutions, associated risks and costs can be made before you commit to invest.
Thorough and robust planning is fundamental to the success of any business.
Team Value ® consultants can provide you with all the support and assistance you need to help and guide you as you develop your business strategy and perform necessary evaluations of your plans including :

Evaluating Business Plans

We can prepare and review business plans to be presented in support of an application for funding.

Budgeting and Strategic Planning

Facilitate the annual budgeting process. Assist in developing long-term financial models and projections that are consistent with your organisation's strategic plan.

Business Case Development

Development of business cases using our financial modeling tools. This work includes working with clients and reviewing their existing business cases where they are putting forward plans to raise funds for major capital or investment projects.

Cash Flow Planning and Forecasting

Develop process and multi period models to predict cash flow and identify strategies to minimize deficits.

Capital Investment Alternatives

Evaluate lease versus purchase decisions. Recommend alternatives for financing capital expenditures.

Business Controls, Compliance and Governance

The establishment of a proper framework of business controls and related processes is an absolute key to the foundation for any business that regards itself as a serious player and fully compliant with both local and international best practice.
Team Value ® pwill take you through all aspects of the design and implementation of a robust system of internal controls appropriate to your business encompassing your business objectives, organisation structure, policies, procedures and processes.
In addition we can assist you in establishing your risk based internal audit needs and structure.
We can review internal controls over your organization's cash and investment assets in an effort to improve income from cash and investments consistent with investment criteria established by the board.
Increasingly, in the Nigerian environment, any business that regards itself as a serious player must ensure full compliance with international regulations and best practice in particular as this relates to audited financial statements and corporate governance. This now entails compliance with – inter alia - US GAAP, IFRS and IAS rules and Sarbanes Oxley rules.
Team Value ® consultants can carry out a health check of your recording processes and policies and reporting structures to ensure that your underlying controls fundamentals are robust and in place and compliant with accepted and expected international best practices.

Business Incorporation and Due Diligence

Team Value ® services includes establishing full legal set-up and consultancy for incorporation of new businesses and start-ups and foreign enterprises establishing business in Nigeria, together with all necessary business and tax registrations including VAT, PAYE – payroll taxes, WHT and other local tax compliance. We are also able to give full advice relating to taxation of employees including expatriates.
We will ensure that you obtain all necessary expatriate quotas and provide a full quota management maintenance and returns compliance service. In addition we can provide a full visa service for all foreign staff.
In addition we can carry out due diligence examinations on behalf of overseas businesses planning to invest in Nigeria.

Pre and Post Project review and economic evaluation of projects

Team Value ® experts are well equipped to carry out pre and post audits and reviews of your projects.
The most important phase in the preparation of any project is the development of the initial cost estimates and the economic evaluation. Just one of the key skills of our team of experts is subjecting project assumptions and project costs estimates to rigorous analysis and evaluation.
As important as pre project review is post review or post audit. From the examination of completed or partially complete projects we can review the events that occurred and evaluate not only what happened, but also why those events happened and from these learnings we can determine the correct actions to take to improve the results of future projects.
