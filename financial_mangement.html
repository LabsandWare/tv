Interim Financial Management

At Team Value ® consultants will provide the financial expertise to provide day-to-day support during the set up of your business. This includes setting up of the legal structures; financial systems and controls; executive reporting structures; drafting the financial regulations; determining VAT and other tax policies and agreeing with tax authorities; establishing cash and funds flow forecasting and treasury management procedures.
In varying circumstances it is not always possible for a new business to have all the necessary quality and quantity of financial management in place at a particular time e.g. start ups, major business projects, expansions, systems implementations. This is where Team Value ® can add value and much needed short term support by supplying – on a defined term or contract basis – the required senior and skilled staff to cover any short falls and also to stand in key positions at the start of a venture to ensure proper structures and processes etc are in place from day one.

Elements of financial management include :
  • Financial management and executive decision support reporting procedures
  • Financial forecasting and planning
  • Business planning - constructing plans and critically reviewing existing plans
  • Financial controls and systems development
  • Drafting and agreeing financial regulations
  • Interim manpower supply

We are also able to carry out a financial management overview being a comprehensive review of your organisation's financial management processes including policies, staffing, management and board attitudes towards financial reports and controls
This can also include a review of procedures and recommendations for the integration of computer based systems to improve transaction processing and record keeping and reporting. This includes an analysis of solutions available to your organisation as well as an evaluation of outsourcing options.
Team Value ® can provide experienced consultants or teams with relevant industry experience. Additionally each consultant is able to draw on additional expertise from within our organisation and in that way we ensure that we provide resources that not only meet your main requirement but also delivers considerable added value to your project. We offer flexible engagement options (short or longer durations) for individuals and teams.
Additionally we have significant experience in providing all levels of manpower in a variety of locations and environments. Furthermore we take pride in our commitment to the training and development of our and our client’s manpower resources.

Our expertise is delivered through our range of consultants. These are selected for their sector experience, quality and track record. This enables us to provide our clients with experienced practitioners. Our consultants pride themselves on delivering projects on time and on budget.
Team Value ® consultants deliver a wide range of consulting and executive support services, including all areas of financial management support, high-level strategic planning, business processes, ERP and other integrated software selection implementation, training, and custom development.
We specialise in providing excellent, practical solutions to your business challenges with a direct hands-on approach. Not only do we provide good, workable solutions to your problems but we take pride in ensuring that we actualise and implement our solutions working alongside, and in concert with, your staff and management to ensure full implementation, remediation and training.

These include :

Taxation and Tax Management

Team Value ® is able to provide a full taxation advisory service covering all aspects of Nigeria taxation as it relates to businesses operating in Nigeria.
Our approach is to ensure our clients operate effectively within the law whilst optimising tax cost and cash flow by examining strategies for managing tax costs and tax risk.
This includes establishing all necessary registrations covering PAYE – payroll taxes, VAT, WHT, Company (Corporate) tax covering both Federal and State tax authorities and other local business taxes.
We will prepare your tax computations and annual tax returns and liaise with the FIRS on company tax issues.
In addition, if required, we are able to provide a full regular service for routine tax returns validation, checking and filing of WHT tax returns, VAT returns and obtaining annual tax clearance certificates.
